About Us

Transit Tower is a cozy guesthouse nestled in the heart of Maradhoo in Addu City. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're here for business or leisure, we aim to make your stay as comfortable and memorable as possible.

Our guest house offers a range of contemporary air-conditioned rooms to ensure your comfort during your stay. Indulge in the authentic flavors of the Maldives, Asia, and the West at our on-site restaurant, while staying connected with complimentary Wi-Fi.


Our well-appointed rooms feature flat-screen satellite TV channels and private bathrooms equipped with showers and complimentary toiletries. For an extra touch of luxury, select rooms even boast an open-air shower area, Jacuzzi, and bath tub, providing you with an unforgettable experience.


We believe in going the extra mile to make your stay memorable. Take advantage of our guest services, which include bicycle rental, laundry facilities, and airport transfers (additional fees may apply). For added peace of mind, we offer safety deposit boxes to safeguard your belongings. If you’re an adventure enthusiast, we have you covered with arrangements for diving and snorkeling experiences. And for those eager to explore the wonders of the Maldives, our tour desk is at your service, providing valuable information on exciting day trips, such as whale-shark watching.

Why Choose Us?

Warm Hospitality

The guest house is known for its warm and welcoming hospitality. The staff members are attentive, friendly, and go out of their way to make guests feel comfortable and well taken care of. A friendly and accommodating staff can significantly enhance the overall experience of your stay.

Expanse of Activities

Whether you are an adventure seeker or prefer a relaxing vacation, Transit Tower offers a range of activities to cater to different preferences. From snorkeling and diving in the vibrant coral reefs to dolphin-watching excursions and sunset cruises, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Authentic Region Specific Experience

Staying in a guest house allows you to experience the local culture and way of life more intimately than in large resorts. Transit Tower provides an authentic Maldivian experience, allowing you to interact with locals, savor traditional Maldivian cuisine, and learn about their customs and traditions.
