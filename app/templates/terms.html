Terms & Conditions

1. General Policies

Welcome to Transit Tower! By booking a stay at our guesthouse, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions. These terms ensure a comfortable and secure experience for all our guests.

2. Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in Time: 12:00 PM. Early check-in may be available upon request and is subject to availability. Additional charges may apply.
  • Check-out Time: 12:00 PM. Late check-outs are available for a fee and must be pre-arranged with the front desk.

3. Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Please note our cancellation policy:

  • Free Cancellation: Cancellations made at least 3 days before the arrival date are eligible for a full refund.
  • Late Cancellation Fee: Cancellations made within 3 days of arrival will incur a USD 50 fee.
  • No-Show Policy: If you fail to check in on the arrival date without prior notice, a penalty equivalent to the first night's rate will apply.

4. Pet Policy

For the comfort and safety of all guests, pets are not allowed within the property. Service animals are welcome and accommodated following legal requirements.

5. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash (in USD or EUR only).

All payments must be settled during check-in or prior to the guest's stay.

6. Currency

All transactions are processed in USD or EUR. Guests are advised to confirm the exchange rate with their respective payment providers.
